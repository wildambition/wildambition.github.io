ABOUT

Artist:
Matt Bachman

Songwriter/Guitarist
Americana – Rock
Los Angeles, CA

Matt Bachman’s songwriting influences have been equated to Tom Petty, Brian Adams and Elliot Smith. His guitar style is reminiscent of the players of the 70’s with a modern flare adding a fresh perspective. He tends to dig deep with his lyrical approach with emphasis on real life experiences and lessons he has learned along lives journey. He states “I steer away from words that don’t make me feel something real, I stick with my basic instincts and I’m driven by my passion to create.” Songwriter and friend Katie Ferrara states “Matt is a strong songwriter, his lyrics are genuine yet contain an edge”. Currently, Matt is immersed in songwriting and recording new material. Matt continues to write with talented artists, producers and projects. At the moment he is writing for commercial placements and licensing opportunities. Upon request, Matt is interested in songwriting projects, co-writing and live guitar performance.
